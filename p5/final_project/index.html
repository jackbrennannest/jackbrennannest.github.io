BRICK BREAKER

Instructions:

Each brick has three lives break all the bricks and you move on to the next level. Every few seconds you will get a "powerup". If you hit the brick from the side it will be destroyed immediately. if the ball hits the bottem you loose a life, loose all of your lives and you loose. Break all the bricks and you move on to the next level. If you beat level 4 then you win!
